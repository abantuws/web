Abuntu Wireless is a Wireless Internet Service Provider building next generation wide area broadband wireless networks using television spectrum across Africa, with a primary focus on Sub-Sahara Africa.

What is Whitespace?
Television White Space technology. TV White Space (TVWS), refers to the unlicensed spectrum that exist between 2 terrestrial channels being delivered over the UHF or VHF television bands.
TV White space allows Abantu Whitespace to offer broadband service to users who may not have access to a fiber-optic network or may even be challenged by geographical hindrances, at a fraction of the cost of other broadband services. TV White Space is integral to our last mile solution.
